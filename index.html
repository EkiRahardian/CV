ABOUT ME

 
I am a passionate programmer who enjoys finding new ways to automate things. One of the cases is when I need to generate thousands of images of characters from various writing systems. I know it will be a chore to take screenshots of the characters one at a time, so I tirelessly search for a way to generate them automatically. The result is more than 18.000 images generated and uploaded to Wikimedia Commons.

ACHIEVEMENTS

  • Automatically generated more than 18.000 images, including 6913 images of Tangut characters, 470 images of Khitan small script characters, and 56 images of Batak characters using JavaScript, Unicode codepoints, Batch script, etc., then integrated them into Wikidata using Python and Pywikibot. The characters in question are used to illustrate Unicode characters, especially those not typically represented in fonts.
  • Developed a cipher method that is able to encrypt and decrypt the whole Unicode scripts including uncommon scripts such as Makasar, Hanifi Rohingya, Old Hungarian, Gothic, Tangut, etc. and documented it as an undergraduate thesis.
  • Wrote the undergraduate thesis aforementioned entirely in HTML and Javascript when the office tools like Microsoft Word, Excel, etc. are broken.
